Animals

There are many animals in our parks! From elk to fish to bears, you can see a huge variety of animals! They roam around, not looking for danger, but want to be left alone. These are the three most common animals you can see around our park.

Bear

There are a lot of bears throughout our parks. They are very shy and would like to be left alone, but they try to stay close to home.

Elk

These animals are very skiddish. They are very scared of humans and will try to run away. If you get to hear their bugle then you will be shocked how loud it is!

Ram

These beasts can ascend walls as steep as you can think! They are usually on top of hills and mountains, but are seen many times trying to drink water.
